Privacy Policy - Full Page Screenshot

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no cookies.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions solely to perform its core functionality:

  • activeTab / tabs — to capture the current page
  • scripting — to scroll the page during full-page capture
  • downloads — to save the screenshot to your computer
  • storage — to remember your capture mode preference locally

All data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

Screenshots

Screenshots are saved directly to your local Downloads folder. They are never uploaded anywhere.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: fullpagescreenshot.ext@gmail.com
